About Musicology Study (Music Journalism)

Educational Aim of the Programme

The aim of the BAM programme is to develop practically competent, innovative, self-motivated, critical thinking, reflective, and relevant graduates with generic, productive and entrepreneurship skills which would prepare them for advanced studies; and enable them to produce products and provide services in music and music related industries.

Other high-end abilities to be found in BAM graduates are concise and accurate communication, integrity, project management, team work, paying attention to detail, competence and impactful entrepreneurial best practices.
